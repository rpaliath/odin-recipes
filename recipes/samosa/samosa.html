Samosa

Home

Ingredients

  1. All-purpose flour - 2 cups
  2. Potatoes - 3 medium, boiled and mashed
  3. Green peas - 1/2 cup, boiled
  4. Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  5. Green chili - 1, finely chopped
  6. Ginger - 1 inch piece, grated
  7. Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  8. Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  9. Garam masala - 1/2 tsp
  10. Red chili powder - 1/2 tsp
  11. Salt - to taste
  12. Oil - for frying and 2 tbsp for the dough
  13. Water - as needed for the dough

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, salt, and 2 tbsp of oil. Gradually add water and knead to form a firm dough. Cover and set aside for 30 minutes.

  2. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions, green chili, and grated ginger. Saut√© until onions are translucent.

  3. Add boiled potatoes and green peas to the pan. Mix well.

  4. Add coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.

  5. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a ball. Roll out each ball into a thin oval shape. Cut it in half to form two semi-circles.

  6. Take one semi-circle, fold it into a cone shape, and seal the edge with water. Fill the cone with the potato filling and seal the top edge.

  7. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

  8. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.

Enjoy your homemade samosas!
