Chicken Biriyani


Recipe

Chicken Biryani is a layered chicken and rice dish full of Indian flavors and spices. Biriyani is made by layering marinated chicken
with partially cooked basmati rice, then cooking it all together to allow the flavors to blend.
It is often garnished with fried onions, fresh coriander, and mint leaves, and served with raita or a side salad.

Samosa


Recipe

samosa is a popular Indian snack consisting of a crispy, golden-brown pastry filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes and peas.
The dough is made from all-purpose flour, water, and a bit of oil, rolled into thin circles, and then filled with the flavorful filling.
The filled pastries are deep-fried until they are crisp and golden. Samosas are typically served hot with chutney or sauce.

masala dosa


Recipe

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish consisting of a thin, crispy crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, filled with a spiced potato mixture.
The dosa is cooked on a griddle until golden and crispy, then filled with the flavorful potato filling, which typically includes mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, and green chilies.
It is usually served with coconut chutney and sambar, a lentil-based vegetable stew.
